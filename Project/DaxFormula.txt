Total_Sales_Amount =
 SUMX('Sales Table','Sales Table'[Quantity] * 'Sales Table'[Price])

3.Create a measure to calculate the Total Discount.

Total_Discount =
SUMX(
    'Sales Table',
    'Sales Table'[Quantity] * 'Sales Table'[Discount]
)

Avg_Product_Price =

MAX('Sales Table'[Price])

6.Create a measure to find the Lowest Product Price.
